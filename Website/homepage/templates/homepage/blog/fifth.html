Indulge Your Need For Speed In Singapore!

Monday, September 10, 2012

Singapore has a lot to offer for all kinds of travelers. When you plan a trip to this world class destination you can expect your vacation to be full of surprises from beautiful architecture to great food and amazing theme parks!Apart from the obvious tourist attractions, Singapore has something special to offer for sports enthusiasts! If you have a need for speed, then you should definitely plan a trip to experience 2012 Formula 1 Singapore Grand PrixSingapore is often described as the “Jewel in the Formula One crown” and this year, it looks to raise the bar with an event like none other! 24 of the world’s best drivers will race through Singapore’s Marina Bay Street Circuit at over 200 miles per hour in the world’s only F1 night race.

Apart from the race, this year the entertainment line up is packed with a star studded cast of 300 performers across 5 stages! You can expect to see performances from Katy Perry, Maroon 5 and Asia’s very own R&B king, Jay Chou. So don’t wait any longer, plan your trip to the 2012 Formula 1 Singapore Grand Prix with us
